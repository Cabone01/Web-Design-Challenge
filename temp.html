About the Graph: Latitude vs. Max temperature



In the plot above as you can see we are seeing how the max temperature of the city changes as we move towards and away from the equator. We can see that the maximum temperature has signs of being a bell curve. The reason is that the maximum temperature increases from both directions as we approach the equator.
