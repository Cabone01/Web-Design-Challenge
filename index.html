About the Project: Latitude vs.



For this project, we are looking at how the change in latitude to the equator affects different aspects of weather. All the data was pulled from OpenWeatherMap API. The following graphs were made using the mentioned data and plotted using latitude for each plot. Only four factors were used in the comparison temperature, cloudiness, wind speed, and humidity. Further details of the plots can be found in the navigation or by clicking one of the plots that are to the right.
